Our Story

Crafting Dreams in Gold & Diamonds

Founded in Dubai in 1999 by Fadi Marhaba, Isadora Jewellery was born from a passion for transforming precious materials into wearable art. After six years mastering the craft across Asia Pacific — specialising in diamonds, coloured stones, and fine craftsmanship — Fadi brought his vision to the Gulf region.

Today, Isadora combines cutting-edge CAD technology with traditional artisanship to create pieces that are entirely yours. Every client becomes part of the creation journey, from diamond selection to the final polish.

  • CAD designing & CAM printing for precision
  • 18K gold, natural diamonds & precious stones
  • Full transparency — video & photo updates from our factory
  • One-on-one personalised consultations
  • Prices lower than market with uncompromised quality

How It Works

Your Vision, Our Craft

From your first spark of inspiration to the final masterpiece, we guide you through every step of the bespoke creation process.

01

Consultation

Share your vision with us. We discuss your style, preferences, budget, and the occasion to understand exactly what you are looking for.

02

Design

Our designers create detailed CAD renderings of your piece. You review, refine, and approve every detail before production begins.

03

Crafting

Master jewellers bring your design to life using 18K gold and hand-selected stones. We share video and photo updates throughout.

04

Delivery

Your finished piece is quality-checked, certified, and delivered to you — complete with a lifetime warranty and 30-day guarantee.
